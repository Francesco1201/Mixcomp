ONLINE MIX COMPARATOR

Instructions: Load a track by clicking either "Choose A" or "Choose B". Press button "A" to listen to track A, or "B" to listen to track B. The "A+B" button plays both loaded tracks simultaneously, while "A/B" allows you to seamlessly switch between them for a direct comparison. The "LU" button matches the perceived volume (LUFS) of the two tracks so they sound equally loud without clipping. Finally, the bin icon ("Reset") clears the current session and removes the loaded tracks.
